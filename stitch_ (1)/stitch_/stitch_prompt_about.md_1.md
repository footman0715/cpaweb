Use the existing homepage design system: Ink Forest `#1F3A2E`, Warm Paper `#FBF8F3`, Pale Gold `#C9A961`, Signal Orange `#F5A623` (CTAs only), Noto Sans TC + Inter, editorial restraint (think 天下雜誌 特輯 + Stripe About page), 1px hairlines, whisper-soft shadows.

**LANGUAGE:** Traditional Chinese primary; English only in eyebrow labels and credential codes.
